Company Behind

The holding behind Certigy Chain is an important energy player in the central and eastern European region. The current business model builds on supplying electricity and natural gas to approximately 200,000 customer sites across the Central and Eastern regions of the European Union. The portfolio of customers consists of industrials, shopping malls, office buildings, small businesses and households. The company was established in 2000 and from beginning, it focused on a fresh approach to energy sector.

Core Project

The core product of Certigy Chain is implementation of an Electricity and Gas marketplace, with a fully transparent Certificate of Origin, itemized on a blockchain record.

Customers will know precisely where their energy comes from and what they pay for: if their energy bill is for a green energy from wind, hydrocarbon-fuelled black energy, nuclear energy, or for some source of alternative energy from smart grid (biogas power plants, photovoltaic systems, etc.). Similar ledger of Certificates of Origin will be established for supplies of the natural gas. Our platform will take benefit from the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning achievements through enabling more complex, smarter and efficient approach for every user.

Freedom of choice

Existence of a CERTIFICATE OF ORIGIN for every single unit of energy that would be traded in our system brings change of paradigm to markets for power and utilities, as the consumers’ freedom of choice in financial, political, industrial, environmental and global security forces suddenly take central stage. Consumers will choose how much energy, what type of energy, what source of energy they want to pay for, and from where they would like to buy the energy. They will vote on issues of energy policies in their home countries with their e-wallets.

WHY BLOCKCHAIN?

Our decentralized energy exchange platform will be a dual-token driven ecosystem. CEG, the ‘outside world’ token allowing access to the platform and REFLEX*, the platform internal token that represents the amount of energy owned by user. The whole architecture of software will be designed to take advantage of smart contracts, which will allow for full automatization, lack of transaction manipulation, cost reduction and standardisation of processes. The private Blockchain will be the base for transaction processes and a private registration system.
